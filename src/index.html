Our Process Is Science Based

Science

All of our hops are created with science. We go through an exhaustive process involving every detail of the hop.

Grow

After we've done our research, we been to grow our hops in a controlled environment. Every aspect is controlled. From the heat & humidity to the sunlight.

Beer

After our amazing hops are grown and harvested, we ship them to the finest brewers in the world who make the best beer.

Our philosophy is simple:

Design, grow, and harvest the best product we can. We never have and never will accept a product that isn't perfect in every possible way.
